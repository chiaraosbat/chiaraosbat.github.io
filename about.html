I am an Adviser at the Directorate General Economics of the European Central Bank. I started my career at the ECB in 2000, after my PhD studies at the European University Institute. After working many years on exchange rates and competitiveness I now focus on inflation. I coordinated the work of expert groups of the European System of Central Banks on low inflation (2017) and exchange rate pass-through (forthcoming).

These are my personal pages: any views expressed here are my own and do not necessarily reflect those of the European Central Bank or the Eurosystem.
