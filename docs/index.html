I am an Adviser at the European Central Bank. My current research focuses on using micro data to answer macro questions, especially on inflation dynamics and forecasting.

I work with both traditional and newer granular datasets, including scanner and web-scraped data, to study price-setting behaviour, inflation dynamics, and the information micro evidence can add to macro analysis. In previous work, I have contributed to ECB research and policy work on exchange rate determination, exchange rate pass-through, and competitiveness.
